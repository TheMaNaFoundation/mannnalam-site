Meet the Executive Team

Mann Nalam's executive team is entirely youth-led, reflecting our commitment to empowering young voices in philanthropy and community service. Together, they oversee programs, partnerships, and initiatives that directly support farmers and families facing crises, ensuring every effort is thoughtful, effective, and inspiring.

Iniya Nandakumar

Co-Founder

Iniya Nandakumar is an 11th grade student studying in the United States. Coming from a family engaged in both agriculture and politics, she has long been passionate about advocating for Tamil Nadu’s organic farmers. Outside of MaNa, she is active in her school’s speech and debate team and also champions for public education funding in the U.S.

Suryan Saravanan

Co-Founder

Suryan Saravanan is an 11th grade student from the United States. With a strong passion for agriculture and computer science, he seeks to implement technological solutions to improve the lives of struggling farmers, particularly in Tamil Nadu. Outside of MaNa, he’s involved as a software developer for America’s first online Tamil school, where he contributes to promoting the Tamil language and its culture.

Anvitha Desaraju
